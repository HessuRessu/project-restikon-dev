# Restikon Reborn

An agent-centric village builder / colony simulation where every villager is individually simulated.

This is not a traditional colony builder driven by abstract systems. The village exists because its people act: they gather, haul, build, store, and survive - together.

![Seasons](media/seasons/2026-04-10-Seasons-1.png)

## Core Idea

The simulation is built around agents, not buildings.

- Every villager has needs (food, warmth, endurance)
- Work emerges from tasks, not scripted behavior
- Logistics and storage determine whether systems actually function
- Failure is systemic: if supply chains break, the village breaks

## Systems Overview

- **Resource Management**  
  Gathering, processing, and consuming resources is fully simulated

- **Logistics & Hauling**  
  Resources must be physically transported through the world

- **Storage & Capacity**  
  Availability depends on storage, not just totals

- **Agent Needs**  
  Hunger, warmth, and endurance directly affect behavior and survival

- **Settlement Expansion**  
  Villages grow organically based on available labor and resources

- **Biome Characteristics**  
  Each biome defines constraints: varying resource availability and survival pressure

## Design Direction

- Large-scale simulation (hundreds → thousands of agents)
- Real-time, job-based systems
- Multiplayer-ready architecture
- Systems-first development, visual polish later
